Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our WhatsApp Appointment Scheduling Chatbot ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our Service:

  • Phone Number: We receive your phone number to identify you and send replies.
  • Message Content: We process the text of your messages to understand your requests (e.g., appointment dates, times).
  • Usage Data: We track the frequency and timing of your messages to ensure the security and stability of our Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Appointment Scheduling: To book, reschedule, or cancel appointments as requested by you.
  • Service Security: To detect and prevent spam, prank messages, and abusive behavior.
  • Communication: To send you appointment confirmations and reminders.

3. Data Retention

We value your privacy and limit the retention of your data:

  • Appointment Data: Information related to your appointment requests and our replies is stored for a maximum of 30 days. After this period, it is deleted from our active chatbot logs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties, except as necessary to provide the Service (e.g., WhatsApp/Meta infrastructure) or as required by law.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
